About the Project

A Gated Community
Nurtured by Nature

Mayur City by ADM Developers is a RERA-registered affordable plotted colony under Deen Dayal Jan Awas Yojna. Granted Licence No. 144 of 2024 by D.T.C.P. Chandigarh, Haryana, it sits strategically in Sector-27, Jhajjar with access to 9 State & National Highways.

Spread across 18.325 acres with 311 plots ranging from 94.539 to 177.342 sq. yards, it offers curated green landscapes, wide internal roads (45M, 24M, 12M), and luxurious modern amenities — making it a complete world in itself.
