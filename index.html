About the Project

A Gated Community Nurtured by Nature

Mayur City by ADM Developers is a complete world in itself — a RERA-registered affordable plotted colony under the prestigious Deen Dayal Jan Awas Yojna. Strategically located in Sector-27, Jhajjar, Haryana, it offers plot sizes from 94.539 to 177.342 sq. yards with curated lush green landscapes and modern amenities.

The project is granted Licence No. 144 of 2024 by D.T.C.P Chandigarh and is well connected to 9 State & National Highways, making it an ideal investment for families and professionals alike.
